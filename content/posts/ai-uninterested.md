I'm not interested in your LLM-assisted coding workflow. I'm bored by it, in the same way that I would be bored by someone walking me through every extension they have installed for their IDE. *I don't care*.

- I don't care about *agent skills*.

- I don't care about whether to say "please/thank you" to a fucking LLM.

- I don't care about the newest models and how minutely incrementally better it does your job for you.

- I just don't fucking care.

I like learning things and knowing things. I'm good at it. Maybe you aren't. That's why you need these things to do your job for you. Maybe you're too busy to do your job. Maybe you're busy learning these useless no-code skills and becoming a tool yourself.

Maybe you "just want to make things", and "always hated writing code". Fantastic! That's like a baker saying they just love cake, hate ovens and cooking, and would rather buy them from the store. Congratulations, fatass.

Go sit on the couch and prompt your LLM. I'm over here honing my skills and will be ready to do all your work for you once you forget how to make anything.
